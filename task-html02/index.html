My Favorite Movie

Harry Potter series by J.K. Rowling

The Harry Potter film series is a fantasy-adventure story about a young orphan boy named Harry Potter who discovers on his eleventh birthday that he is actually a wizard. He is invited to attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where he learns magic and forms lasting friendships with Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger. As Harry attends school and uncovers more about his family history, he discovers that the evil wizard Lord Voldemort, who killed his parents, is still alive and seeking to regain power. Harry and his friends must face numerous challenges and battles against Voldemort and his followers, known as Death Eaters, to protect the wizarding world from darkness and destruction. Over the course of the eight-film series, Harry grows from a timid boy into a brave and skilled wizard, while facing loss, love, and friendship along the way.
